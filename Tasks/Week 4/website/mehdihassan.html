ABOUT MEHDI HASSAN

★★★★★

RATINGS & REVIEWS

Mehdi Hasan Khan, was a Pakistani ghazal singer and playback singer for Lollywood. One of the greatest and most influential figures in the history of ghazal singing, he is famously known as the "King of Ghazal" or the "Shahanshah-e-Ghazal". Recent Articles

CAREER ACHIEVEMENT

  • 1. Nigar Award for Best Male Playback Singer for Farangi (1964).
  • 2. Nigar Award for Best Male Playback Singer for Saiqa (1968).
  • 3. Nigar Award for Best Male Playback Singer for Zarqa (1969).
  • 4. Nigar Award for Best Male Playback Singer for Meri Zindagi Hai Naghma (1972).
  • 5. Nigar Award for Best Male Playback Singer for Naya Rasta (1973).
  • 6. Nigar Award for Best Male Playback Singer for Sharafat (1974).
  • 7. Nigar Award for Best Male Playback Singer for Zeenat (1975).
  • 8. Nigar Award for Best Male Playback Singer for Shabana (1976).
  • 9. Nigar Award for Best Male Playback Singer for Aaina (1977).
  • 10. Nigar Award Special Millennium Award (1999).
