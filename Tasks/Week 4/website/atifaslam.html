ABOUT ATIF ASLAM

☆★★★★

RATINGS & REVIEWS

Atif Aslam is one of, if not the biggest pop star of Pakistan. Originally the vocalist of the band Jal, Atif launched a booming solo career in 2004 and has since produced many chart topping tracks in the local as well the Indian music industry since. Known for his vocal belting technique, Atif is also the youngest recipient of the Tamgha-e-Imtiaz, Pakistan's foremost civilian award. Recent Articles
