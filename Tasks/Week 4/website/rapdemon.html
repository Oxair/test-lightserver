Abdullah Malik is a young Rapper/Singer/Producer/Lyricist from Islamabad,Pk who covers the genres from Hip Hop/Rap/R&B and even pop to some extent.

I started writing rhymes and producing at the age of 11 taking my main influence as Hip Hop/Rap and then started to cover the other genres too.Tupac Amaru Shakur,Biggie Smalls,Machine Gun Kelly,Rakim,Lowkey,Ezzy E,DMX,Immortal technique,,Micheal Jackson,Eminem and Bohemia are my main inspirations.
