ABOUT HADIQA KIYANI

☆☆☆★★

RATINGS & REVIEWS

Hadiqa Kiani, active since 1995, is one of the biggest pop stars of Pakistan. Winning accolades for singing since her childhood, Kiani has was awarded the highest Pakistan civilian award “Tamgha-e-Imtiaz” for her contributions to the field of music in 2006. She has released five albums to date spanning a myriad of genres from hip-hop to pop and rock. "Wajd" (2017), a turning point of her career and her most recent album sees Hadiqa delve into Sufi and covering folk songs in multiple languages. Recent Articles
